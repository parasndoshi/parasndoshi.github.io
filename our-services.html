Our Services

Comprehensive architectural solutions tailored to your needs

What We Offer

At TDA Architecture, we provide a full spectrum of architectural and design services. From initial concept development to final construction oversight, our integrated approach ensures your project is delivered with excellence at every stage.

Commercial Architecture

We design innovative commercial spaces that enhance productivity, reflect brand identity, and create lasting impressions. Our portfolio includes office buildings, retail centers, hospitality venues, and mixed-use developments.

  • Office Building Design
  • Retail & Shopping Centers
  • Hotels & Hospitality
  • Mixed-Use Developments

Residential Design

We craft dream homes that perfectly balance aesthetics, functionality, and lifestyle. Whether it's a custom single-family residence or a multi-family development, we create spaces that feel like home.

  • Custom Home Design
  • Luxury Villas & Estates
  • Multi-Family Housing
  • Home Renovations & Extensions

Interior Design

Our interior design services transform spaces into beautiful, functional environments. We handle everything from space planning and material selection to furniture design and decorative elements.

  • Space Planning & Layout
  • Material & Finish Selection
  • Custom Furniture Design
  • Lighting Design

Sustainable Design

We are committed to creating environmentally responsible buildings. Our sustainable design services incorporate green building principles, energy-efficient systems, and renewable materials.

  • LEED Certification Consulting
  • Energy Modeling & Analysis
  • Green Building Materials
  • Renewable Energy Integration

Project Management

Our comprehensive project management services ensure your project stays on schedule, within budget, and meets the highest quality standards. We coordinate all aspects of the construction process.

  • Construction Administration
  • Budget Management
  • Schedule Coordination
  • Quality Control

Consultation Services

We offer expert consultation services for clients at any stage of their project. Whether you need feasibility studies, site analysis, or design reviews, our team provides valuable insights and recommendations.

  • Feasibility Studies
  • Site Analysis & Planning
  • Design Review & Critique
  • Code Compliance Review

Our Process

1

Discovery & Consultation

We begin by understanding your vision, needs, and budget through detailed consultations and site analysis.

2

Concept Development

Our team develops initial design concepts, exploring various possibilities and presenting creative solutions.

3

Design Development

We refine the chosen concept, developing detailed drawings, specifications, and 3D visualizations.

4

Documentation

We prepare comprehensive construction documents, ensuring all details are clearly communicated.

5

Construction Oversight

We provide ongoing support during construction, ensuring the design is executed to the highest standards.

6

Project Completion

We conduct final inspections and walkthroughs, ensuring everything meets your expectations before handover.
